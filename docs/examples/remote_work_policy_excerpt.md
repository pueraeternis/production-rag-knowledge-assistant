# Remote and Hybrid Work Policy (excerpt)

> Representative excerpt from `knowledge/policies/remote_work_policy.md`. Fictional company content for retrieval benchmarking — not real HR policy.

## Purpose

Remote and Hybrid Work Policy establishes mandatory requirements for HR stakeholders at AcmeCloud Analytics. This policy supports consistent operations across Gatehouse-protected systems and regional cells.

## Scope

Applies to all employees, contractors, and partners with access to AcmeCloud systems referenced in `policies/remote_work_policy.md`. Exceptions require written approval from the policy owner and HR or Legal where applicable.

## Requirements

Employees may request remote or hybrid work when they meet all of the following eligibility requirements:

- Completed probation period (90 days) with satisfactory performance;
- Role is classified as remote-eligible by HR and the hiring manager;
- Primary work location remains in an approved country (US or EU member state where AcmeCloud is registered);
- Employee maintains a dedicated workspace meeting equipment and bandwidth standards;
- No active performance improvement plan.

Hybrid employees may work from home up to **three days per week** by default. Teams may approve up to **four days per week** with director approval. Fully remote employees work from an approved home office five days per week and visit a hub office at least twice per year for team events.

Remote and hybrid employees must be available during **core hours: 10:00–15:00 local time** on their primary workday timezone, Monday through Friday.
