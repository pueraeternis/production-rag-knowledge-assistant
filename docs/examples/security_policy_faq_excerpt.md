# Information Security Policy — FAQ (excerpt)

> Representative excerpt from `knowledge/policies/security_policy.md`. Fictional company content for retrieval benchmarking.

## Frequently asked questions

### How often must I rotate passwords?

Employees must **rotate passwords every 90 days** for systems not protected by SSO. SSO-backed systems rely on Gatehouse password policies with **minimum 14 characters** and breach list checks. Password reuse across systems is prohibited.

### Is multi-factor authentication required?

**Multi-factor authentication is required** for Gatehouse, email, VPN, GitHub, production consoles, and all customer data stores. Hardware security keys are required for administrators and production deployers.

### Can I use personal USB drives on my laptop?

**Personal USB drives are prohibited** on company laptops. Approved encrypted USB devices may be issued by IT for air-gapped transfers with Security ticket approval. Unauthorized media triggers endpoint alerts.

### How do I report phishing?

Employees must **report suspected phishing to security@acmecloud.io** and use the Outlook "Report Phish" button. Do not click links or forward suspicious messages. Security acknowledges reports within **4 business hours**.

## Scope

Company-wide information security requirements for all AcmeCloud Analytics employees, contractors, and partners with system access. All corporate and customer data handled through Gatehouse-protected systems, laptops, and approved SaaS tools. SOC 2 Type II controls apply.
